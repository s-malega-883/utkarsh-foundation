Small Effort Makes A Big Change

Together to make world a better place...

The Utkarsh Foundation is a non-profit organisation with a mission to build the largest network of innovative schools that empowers children to maximise their potential and that influences systemic reform.

Utkarsh wants to educate children of low income communities across India and thus build up the gap between the "rich" and the "poor".

Looking ahead, we seek to expand our network of schools, share our effective practices and advocate for quality school reform for children across India.
